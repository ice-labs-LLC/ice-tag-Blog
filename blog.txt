Ice Tag Gets a Realism Boost with New Shader

Ice Tag just got a major visual upgrade thanks to a new custom shader developed by Ice Labs LLC.

This shader makes ice look more realistic than ever, with dynamic reflections, detailed textures, and lighting that reacts naturally as you move. Surfaces now show subtle imperfections like scratches and frost, bringing the environment to life.

Even better, it’s optimized for smooth performance, so the game looks sharper without sacrificing speed.

This update isn’t just about visuals—it improves how players read and move through the game, making every slide and tag feel more precise.

And this is just the beginning—Ice Labs has more improvements on the way.
